Work Process

Certified Expert

My goal is to organize the workflow in a way that not only leaves you satisfied with the result, but also makes you want to recommend me to your friends and colleagues.

Flexible Payment System Minimizing Risks

I dont require the full payment upfront. To make our collaboration comfortable, we start with a small advance for the prototype or design concept to ensure we're on the same page. The main payment is distributed across the development stages, minimizing risks and making the process more transparent.

Transparent Development Using Convenient Tools

I carry out the work in user-friendly visual editors like Figma, Webflow, and Draftbit, where you can monitor progress in real time, leave comments, and suggest edits. This reduces misunderstandings and speeds up the approval process.

Long-Term Collaboration and Support

Im committed not only to launching your project, but also to helping with its ongoing development. Most of my clients stay for support and scaling. After the project is delivered, you get two free rounds of revisions for adjustments and bug fixes. If any additional tasks arise, Im always ready to assist. Should any errors occur due to my oversight, I correct them free of charge.

Team Training on Working with the Product

After completing the project, I dont just hand over the website or application I also train your team on how to work with it. I conduct onboarding sessions where I clearly explain how to manage the platform, add content, configure functionality, and handle basic technical tasks. To ensure you can work independently, I also prepare video tutorials, guides, and detailed documentation covering all the key points.

Testing and Quality Assurance at Every Stage

Before handing over the final product, I thoroughly test it on all levels. I check the code, performance, security, and the correct functioning of all features across different devices. I also conduct UX testing to ensure that users can interact with the product intuitively. Special attention is given to responsiveness, loading speed, and cross-browser compatibility to make sure the website or application performs flawlessly under any conditions.

Scalability and Future Project Growth

From the start, I design projects with scalability in mind, so that as your business grows, theres no need to rebuild the system from scratch. If your MVP shows strong results, I help evolve it into a full-fledged product with expanded functionality. I plan the architecture to allow easy addition of new modules, integrations, and features without major rework.

Flexible Technologies: Choosing the Optimal Stack

I dont stick to a fixed set of tools instead, I select technologies based on the specific needs of the project. If fast launch is a priority, I use Webflow, Tilda, or WordPress. If custom functionality and high performance are required, I choose Next.js and React. For mobile applications, I work with React Native, Capacitor, and Ionic. This approach allows me to create a product that best fits your business needs without unnecessary costs or overloaded solutions.

Lets talk about your project?

You can reach out to me via email or through messengers. We'll discuss your project, define your goals, and I'll help find the best solutions tailored specifically to your needs.
